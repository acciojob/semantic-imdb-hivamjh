About movie

SITA RAMAM movie was the based on the love story and also hindu mushlim topic on jamu&kashmir An orphan soldier, Lieutenant Ram's life changes, after he gets a letter from a girl named Sita. He meets her and love blossoms between them. When he comes back to his camp in Kashmir,After he gets caught in jail, he sends a letter to Sita which won't reach her.

Details about the actors:

  • Dulquer Salmaan as Lieutenant Ram.
  • Rashmika Mandanna as Waheeda / Afreen Ali.
  • Sachin Khedekar as Pakistani Major (later Brigadier) Abu Tariq Ali, Afreen's Grandfather.
